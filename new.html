We know our work.

Chitkara University is a big educational institute engaged in providing food services at different locations to its students and employees. The project mainly focuses on all the hostel messes of the university. Currently, the hostel manager is using a manual procedure like registers to manage all the cafeteria operations. At the end of the day in hostel, they spend a couple of hours counting inventory and placing orders for the following week. The mess material management system is designed to not only assist in this problem, but also automate many of the tedious tasks associated with it. The purpose is to have a new comprehensive and flexible centralized material management for Chitkara hostel mess. It will automate the different processes for improving the efficiency of system and ensuring transparency in it.
